About Repu8

Repu8 is a mission lead and data driven company which started as an answer to a universal problem. We realized that people had trouble transferring their career reputation when changing their jobs. A typical hiring process which involves multiple rounds of interviews, forces everyone to start from scratch irrespective of past contributions, years of experience etc.

Everyday, recruiters/m anagers face friction when hiring due to lack of trustable data on candidates. We realized that reputable candidates were being ignored for factors that are not related to their performance or their abilities.

We decided to change that. We made it our mission to democratise building of people's career reputation, remove ambiguities for companies when hiring and to finally help people grow in their careers.
